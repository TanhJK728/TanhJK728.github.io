# Why I Started This Notebook

I started this site as a place to organize my thoughts on machine learning, stochastic processes, trading systems, and research ideas that do not yet fit into a formal paper or project.

This site will mainly include three types of notes:

1. Research notes on AI, JEPA, stochastic processes, and machine learning.
2. Technical writeups from projects I am building or studying.
3. Trading reviews, strategy notes, and reflections from daily market observation.

The goal is to keep a public notebook: clear enough to be useful, updated often, and structured enough to help me build ideas over time.

# 为什么要写这个Notebook

我创建这个网站，是为了整理自己关于机器学习、随机过程、交易系统，以及一些暂时还不适合写成正式论文或项目的研究想法。

这个网站主要会包含三类内容：

1. 关于 AI、JEPA、随机过程和机器学习的研究笔记。
2. 我正在构建或学习的项目中的技术记录。
3. 交易复盘、策略笔记，以及日常市场观察中的反思。

我的目标是维护一个公开的 notebook：内容足够清晰，能对自己和读者有用；更新足够频繁，记录真实的思考过程；结构也足够明确，帮助我长期积累和发展想法。
